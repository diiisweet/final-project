Is Fast Food
Poison?

Is a question millions of people want to have an answer for.
We can speculate about it as long as we want, but it's better to
explore the topic and see all of the advantages and disadvantages.

Pros

  • Requires no cooking
  • Fast food is usually rather cheap
  • Helpful for stressful days
  • Huge variety of different fast foods
  • Integrated Ecosystem

Cons

  • Unhealthy ingredients with many Calories
  • Too much of it can lead to serious health issues
  • May change our social life
  • Fast food addictions
  • Problems with your digestive system



Main Points are:


  • increased risk of heart diseases due to high cholesterol levels.

  • fast food is associated with obesity.

  • result in decreased energy levels and overall well-being.
